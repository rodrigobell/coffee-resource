SHORT BLACK - demitasse cup (70ml)

This is the base of all espresso beverages

Place a demitasse cup under the group head and extract a double shot. Serve immediately!

RISTRETTO- demitasse cup (70ml)

The most concentrated of all espresso beverages, the ristretto is a ‘restricted’ short black

Extract shot, restricting the pour and stopping the extraction 2/3 of the way. Serve immediately!

LONG BLACK - tulip cup (150ml)

The extraction is poured on top of the water so the crema is not disturbed

Fill cup just over half full with hot water aneextract shot over the hot water.

AMERICANO - cappuccino cup (190ml)

Like the Long Black, but in a larger cup which makes it more difficult to retain a crema

Extract shot over hot water.

MACCHIATO – demitasse cup (70ml)

A short black ‘marked’ with hot milk

Extract shot and add the mark of milk to the espresso by spoon.

FLAT WHITE – tulip cup (150ml) or cappuccino cup (190ml)

Ensure the milk has very little froth

Extract a double shot in to the cup, then ease the smooth flat steamed milk into the centre of the coffee.

LATTE – glass (220ml) or cup (250ml)

The texture of the milk in a latte can be slightly thicker

Pour and allow the thicker, lightly textured milk to flow in to and mix with the espresso.

CAPPUCCINO – cappuccino cup (190ml)

A double shot with a very thick, head of frothy milk

Hold back the heavily textured milk and pour approximately 1⁄2 a cup of lightly textured milk in to the double shot. Use the spoon to push the remaining, heavily textured top milk in to the remainder of the cup.

MOCCACCINO – latte cup (250ml)

Mix as shots are being pulled

A double shot of espresso with chocolate powder, combined with lightly textured milk.

HOT CHOCOLATE (milk should be made as per Latte)

Use your pre-made chocolate syrup or add 3 heaped tablespoons of chocolate powder to a cup

Pour the textured milk in to the espresso/choc mix. Sprinkle with plenty of chocolate powder.

Note: All drinks should be made with a double shot of espresso, unless otherwise requested
